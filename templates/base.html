A Note on Custom Deployments

We believe in doing one thing flawlessly rather than many things adequately. Because of this, Himalayan Hives currently operates exclusively on 2 Master Itineraries. We pour all our resources and local expertise into perfecting these specific routes. While we know every hidden trail listed above, we only accept custom trips to other regions on exceedingly rare occasions when our core calendar allows. We deeply appreciate your understanding of our commitment to quality.
